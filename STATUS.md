## Technical Specifications

### Chapter Format
- **HTML Source:** Professional semantic HTML5
- **CSS Styling:** Embedded styles matching Chapter 1 design
- **Typography:** Georgia 14pt body, 42pt/28pt/20pt headers
- **Colors:** ModelIt brand (#0F6ACE primary, #48d2fc accent)
- **Target PDF Size:** 8-12 MB per chapter with images

### Image Requirements
- **Per Chapter:** 6-8 educational illustrations
- **Format:** PNG, base64-embedded in PDF
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI minimum
- **Style:** Educational, appropriate for grades 5-8
- **Generator:** Gemini 2.5 Flash ("Nano Banana")

### Standards Compliance
- **NGSS:** All chapters aligned to relevant MS standards
- **Reading Level:** Grades 5-8 (verified with Flesch-Kincaid)
- **Accessibility:** High contrast, semantic HTML, screen-reader compatible
